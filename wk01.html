Week 1: Jan 23 - Jan 29

Last Updated: Wed Jan 17 12:42:50 PM

Learning Objectives

  • Be familiar with modern team collaboration and version control tools
  • Write and parse R functions
  • Use R vectors efficiently
  • Compare and contrast loops and mapping functions

Prepare

  • Join our Slack Channel csuc-ads-s18
  • Install the Slack app for your phone and/or computer
  • Update R & R Studio (if done < 6mo ago)
  • If you’re new to Git - I recommend using Sourcetree as your external client (yes you have to make an Atlassian account, but that’s ok. It opens availability to their other useful software products as well)
  • Get the textbook - Weapons of Math Destruction. There is 1 copy available in the Library – Someone go check this out for the semester and share!

The Slack phone app is technically optional, but really really helpful/handy. Students historically have stated it’s much easier to work with. If you opt out of the phone app, make sure the program is installed onto your laptop. You need to be connected throughout the semester, not just when you remember to log in.
